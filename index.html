02Simple 3-step process

Scan. Review. Act.

PrivyScan is built for users who aren't security experts — clear results, plain language, and actions you can actually take.

1
Tap "Scan All Apps"
PrivyScan reads every installed app's granted permissions, checks for known tracker SDKs, and merges live dynamic data from the monitor — all in under 30 seconds.
2
Review your Risk Score
See a privacy score from 0–100, a list of HIGH and MEDIUM risk apps, hidden trackers found, and any background activity detected since monitoring started.

03Risk classification

Three clear risk levels

Every app gets a hybrid score based on permissions, trackers, and real-time behaviour. No vague warnings — just a clear verdict.

≥ 7.0
HIGH Risk
App holds multiple sensitive permissions, known tracking SDKs, or was detected accessing camera/mic/location in the background. Immediate action recommended.
4.0–6.9
MEDIUM Risk
App has notable permissions or trackers but no confirmed background abuse. Review what it has access to and revoke anything you don't need it to have.
< 4.0
LOW Risk
App has minimal permissions, no known trackers detected, and no suspicious background activity observed. No action needed.
04Installation guide

Install in 4 steps

PrivyScan is distributed as an APK outside the Play Store. Android requires one extra permission step — here's exactly what to do.

1
Download the APK
Tap the Download button or scan the QR code. The APK file will save to your device's Downloads folder.
2
Open the APK file
Open your Files app, go to Downloads, and tap PrivyScan.apk. Android will ask for permission to install from unknown sources.
3
Allow "Install unknown apps"
Tap Settings on the prompt → toggle Allow from this source → press Back → tap Install.
⚠️ This only applies to the Files app. You can turn it off again after installation.
4
Grant Accessibility Service (optional)
For real-time monitoring, go to Settings → Accessibility → PrivyScan Monitor and enable it. This lets PrivyScan detect background camera/mic/location use.
